# Sales Management Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes sales, customers, employees, products, and payment data to generate actionable business insights.

The dashboard enables management to monitor customer behavior, product performance, employee information, and sales transactions from a single interactive report.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer distribution
- Monitor product performance
- Understand employee hierarchy
- Track order activity
- Analyze payment information
- Generate business insights

---

## Dataset

The project uses multiple relational tables:

- Customers
- Orders
- Order Details
- Products
- Product Lines
- Employees
- Offices
- Payments

---

## Data Model

Relationships include:

- Customers → Orders
- Orders → Order Details
- Products → Order Details
- Product Lines → Products
- Employees → Customers
- Offices → Employees
- Customers → Payments

---

- Product Line Revenue

### Employee Dashboard

KPIs:

Visuals:

- Employee Directory

---

## Key Insights

- USA has the largest customer base.
- Classic Cars is the highest-selling product line.
- Trains generate the lowest sales volume.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-value customers.
- The company operates across 27 countries with 122 customers.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs.
- Increase marketing for low-performing product lines.
- Expand into underrepresented regions.
- Measure employee performance using sales KPIs.
- Improve payment tracking and customer segmentation.
